WAYS TO HELP

Here are some ways you could help us:

AMAZON.COM

Make purchases under Amazon Smile and a portion of your order will be donated back to Riverside Art Center. It's easy to sign up and easy to support RAC! Go to smile.amazon.com and choose Riverside Art Center to support.

KROGER

Give to RAC by going to Kroger rewards site and sign in. Then go to My Account in the upper right-hand corner, click on that edit the Kroger Community Rewards button, put in the number for Riverside: 49146, then click on the circle for Riverside and you're done! It's really easy RAC benefits.

CASH

If you have Community Market receipts, please bring them to Riverside Art Center, Community Market will support us with 2% of your non-acholic purchases. These funds support supplies to sustain our art center.
