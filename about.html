Who are we?

The Riverside Art Center is a not-forprofit Arts Council, with an eleven member Board of Trustees. We are incorporated, and have a Federal Tax ID. We have obtained a 501(c)(3) Status with the State of Ohio, as well as received grants from Ohio Arts Council, who remain supporters. Riverside strives to promote all forms of artistic expression, with focus on the visual arts.

We are a community minded organization, offering innovative art events and activites for all ages. We are family oriented and open to all surrounding communities. The center is dedicated to providing a wide range of programs that are not only entertaining and educational, but also benefit participants through the satisfaction of their personal accomplishments! We give people a reason to be excited about the Arts!
