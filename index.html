TO THE RIVERSIDE ART CENTER

Riverside Art Center, a 501c3 organization, was founded to inspire creativity through the arts. Located in beautiful & historic downtown Wapakoneta, OH.

Learn More

MISSION STATEMENT

  • The Riverside Art Center (RAC) is a non-profit arts corporation dedicated to the promotion of fine art.

  • RAC serves the greater Wapakoneta area, providing art related, educational opportunities through classes, lectures and demonstrations.

  • RAC provides a venue for local artists to work, display and sell their artwork.

  • The RAC provides these opportunities for all persons, regardless of age, social status, gender/gender orientation, politics, religious affiliation.
